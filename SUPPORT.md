## 💡 Getting the Most from Support

### Before Contacting Support

1. **Check documentation** - 90% of questions are answered there
2. **Search existing issues** - Your question may already be answered
3. **Try troubleshooting guide** - Common problems have solutions
4. **Gather information** - Version, logs, steps to reproduce

### When Contacting Support

**Do:**
✅ Be specific and detailed  
✅ Include version and environment info  
✅ Provide steps to reproduce  
✅ Attach logs or screenshots  
✅ Be patient and respectful  

**Don't:**
❌ Demand immediate responses  
❌ Post sensitive data publicly  
❌ Cross-post same question everywhere  
❌ Be rude or aggressive  

### Escalation Path

1. **Self-service** → Documentation, FAQ
2. **Community** → GitHub Discussions
3. **Email support** → support@your-company.com
4. **Phone support** → Enterprise customers only
5. **Management escalation** → escalations@your-company.com
