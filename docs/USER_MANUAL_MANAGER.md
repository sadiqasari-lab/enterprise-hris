## Team Management

### Viewing Your Team

1. Go to **My Team** from sidebar
2. See all direct reports with:
   - Current status (Present/Absent/On Leave)
   - Position and department
   - Recent activity
3. Click any team member to view their full profile

**What You Can See:**
- Attendance history
- Leave balances and requests
- Performance goals
- Documents (if relevant to management)

**What You Cannot See:**
- Salary details (confidential - HR only)
- Personal documents (ID, certificates)
- Payslips

### Team Attendance Overview

1. Navigate to **Team** → **Attendance**
2. View options:
   - **Calendar View**: Daily team presence
   - **List View**: Detailed check-in/out times
   - **Summary View**: Weekly/monthly statistics
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Status (Present, Absent, Late)
   - Team member

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- 🔴 Repeated late check-ins
- 🔴 Frequent absences without leave
- 🔴 Unusual patterns

**Action Steps:**
1. Click on flagged record
2. Add a note/comment
3. Speak with team member privately
4. Escalate to HR if pattern continues

## Leave Approvals

### Reviewing Leave Requests

1. You'll see pending requests in:
   - Dashboard notifications (bell icon)
   - **Leave Approvals** sidebar section
   - Email notifications
2. Click **Review** to see details:
   - Employee name and position
   - Leave type and duration
   - Working days (excludes weekends)
   - Current leave balance
   - Reason provided
   - Team calendar showing who else is on leave

### Approving a Request

1. Click **Approve** button
2. Add optional comment (e.g., "Enjoy your vacation!")
3. Confirm approval
4. System automatically:
   - Deducts days from employee's balance
   - Sends notification to employee
   - Updates team calendar

### Rejecting a Request

1. Click **Reject** button
2. **You must provide a reason** (required)
   - Example: "Peak project period - please reschedule"
3. Confirm rejection
4. Employee receives notification with your reason

**Best Practices:**
- ✅ Review within 24 hours
- ✅ Check team calendar for overlaps
- ✅ Maintain minimum team coverage
- ✅ Be fair and consistent
- ❌ Don't delay decisions
- ❌ Don't reject without clear reason

### Managing Team Coverage

Before approving leave:

1. Check **Team Calendar** view
2. Ensure adequate coverage:
   - Critical projects have staffing
   - Skills are not single-pointed
   - Peak periods are covered
3. Consider:
   - Client deadlines
   - Ongoing projects
   - Team morale

**Rule of Thumb:** No more than 30% of team on leave simultaneously.

## Performance Management

### Setting Goals

**Quarterly/Annual Cycle:**

1. Go to **Performance** → **Goals**
2. Click **Add Goal** for team member
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Clear, specific goal
   - **Description**: Detailed expectations
   - **Target Date**: Realistic deadline
   - **Linked to Cycle**: Q1 2026, Annual 2026, etc.
4. Click **Save**

**SMART Goals Framework:**
- **S**pecific: "Reduce bug count by 50%"
- **M**easurable: Use metrics
- **A**chievable: Realistic targets
- **R**elevant: Aligned with business
- **T**ime-bound: Clear deadline

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Complete AWS certification by March 31"
- ✅ "Mentor 2 junior developers throughout Q1"
- ✅ "Improve code review turnaround to <24h"
- ❌ "Be a better developer" (too vague)

### Tracking Goal Progress

1. **Performance** → **Team Goals**
2. View progress bars (0-100%)
3. Click goal to see details
4. Update progress:
   - Employee can self-update
   - You can override if needed
5. Status options:
   - Not Started
   - In Progress
   - Completed
   - Cancelled

**Monthly Check-ins:**
- Review goal progress with each team member
- Update percentage
- Adjust targets if needed
- Provide feedback

### Conducting Appraisals

**At End of Cycle:**

1. Go to **Performance** → **Appraisals**
2. Select employee and cycle
3. Fill comprehensive form:

**Rating (1-5)**
- 1: Needs Improvement
- 2: Below Expectations
- 3: Meets Expectations
- 4: Exceeds Expectations
- 5: Outstanding

**Written Feedback Sections:**
- **Overall Feedback**: Summary of performance
- **Strengths**: What they excel at
- **Areas for Improvement**: Constructive feedback
- **Development Recommendations**: Training, certifications, etc.

4. Save as **DRAFT** (you can edit later)
5. When ready, click **Submit**
6. Employee receives notification
7. Employee reviews and acknowledges

**Tips for Great Appraisals:**
- ✅ Use specific examples
- ✅ Balance positive and constructive
- ✅ Focus on behaviors, not personality
- ✅ Provide actionable recommendations
- ❌ Don't surprise employees (continuous feedback)
- ❌ Don't compare team members
- ❌ Don't rush - take time to be thoughtful

## Common Manager Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Team Member Repeatedly Late

**Steps:**
1. Review attendance data for patterns
2. Schedule private 1-on-1 meeting
3. Understand root cause (traffic, childcare, etc.)
4. Agree on solution:
   - Flexible start time if possible
   - Work-from-home options
   - Performance improvement plan if needed
5. Document conversation
6. Monitor improvement
7. Escalate to HR if no improvement after 30 days

### Scenario 2: Multiple Leave Requests for Same Week

**Decision Process:**
1. Check project deadlines - any blockers?
2. Review team skills - adequate coverage?
3. Options:
   - **Approve all**: If work allows
   - **Approve partial**: First-come-first-served or rotation
   - **Negotiate**: Ask if dates flexible
4. Communicate decision clearly with reasoning
5. Help team coordinate coverage

### Scenario 3: Underperforming Team Member

**Action Plan:**
1. Review goals and performance data
2. Schedule performance discussion:
   - Come prepared with examples
   - Listen to their perspective
   - Identify obstacles
3. Create improvement plan:
   - Specific actions required
   - Timeline (usually 30-60 days)
   - Support you'll provide (training, mentoring)
   - Consequences if no improvement
4. Document everything
5. Weekly check-ins during PIP period
6. Involve HR early

## Best Practices

### Leave Management
- ✅ Respond to requests within 24 hours
- ✅ Encourage employees to plan leave in advance
- ✅ Maintain team calendar visibility
- ✅ Be consistent in decision-making
- ✅ Document verbal approvals in system

### Performance Management
- ✅ Set goals within first 2 weeks of cycle
- ✅ Monthly 1-on-1s to discuss progress
- ✅ Document achievements in real-time
- ✅ Calibrate ratings across your team
- ✅ Provide development opportunities

### Communication
- ✅ Keep team informed of policy changes
- ✅ Use system for official approvals (not just email)
- ✅ Respect confidentiality
- ✅ Escalate issues appropriately
- ✅ Provide constructive feedback promptly
