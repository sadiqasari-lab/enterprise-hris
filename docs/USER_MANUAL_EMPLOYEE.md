# Employee User Manual

Welcome to the Enterprise HRIS Platform! This guide will help you navigate the system and use its features.

---

## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Visit your company's HRIS portal (e.g., `https://hris.your-company.com`)
2. Enter your **email** and **temporary password** (provided by HR)
3. You'll be prompted to change your password on first login
4. Choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)

### Your Dashboard

After login, you'll see your Employee Dashboard with three main sections:

**1. Quick Actions**
- Check In / Check Out buttons
- Request Leave
- View Recent Activity

**2. Leave Summary**
- Current leave balances (Annual, Sick, Emergency)
- Pending requests status
- Quick link to request new leave

**3. Pending Documents**
- Documents awaiting your signature
- Recently uploaded documents
- Quick access to document library

## Daily Attendance

### How to Check In

1. Click **Check In** button on your dashboard
2. The system will automatically:
   - Capture your current location via GPS
   - Detect office WiFi if you're on premises
   - Request a selfie for verification (if enabled)
3. Confirm the check-in
4. You'll see confirmation with your check-in time

**Important Notes:**
- Check in must be done within the allowed time window (usually 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM)
- You must be within 100 meters of your designated office location
- If using mobile, allow location permissions

### How to Check Out

1. Click **Check Out** button
2. Confirm check-out
3. System shows your total hours worked for the day

**Tip:** Always check out at the end of your day to ensure accurate time tracking!

### Viewing Your Attendance History

1. Navigate to **Attendance** from the sidebar
2. You'll see a calendar view with:
   - ✅ Green badges = Present
   - ❌ Red badges = Absent
   - ⚠️ Yellow badges = Late/Early departure
3. Filter by month to view historical records
4. Download attendance summary for your records

## Leave Management

### Checking Your Leave Balance

1. Go to **Leave** from sidebar
2. View your balance cards showing:
   - **Annual Leave**: 30 days per year (Saudi labor law)
   - **Sick Leave**: 15 days per year
   - **Emergency Leave**: 5 days per year
3. Each card shows:
   - Total allocated days
   - Days used
   - Days remaining

### Requesting Leave

1. Click **Request Leave** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Leave Type**: Select from dropdown
   - **Start Date**: Click calendar icon
   - **End Date**: Click calendar icon
   - **Reason**: Brief explanation (optional but recommended)
3. System automatically calculates **working days** (excludes weekends: Friday-Saturday)
4. Click **Submit Request**

**What Happens Next:**
- Request goes to your manager for approval
- You'll receive a notification when approved/rejected
- If approved, days are automatically deducted from your balance

### Canceling a Leave Request

1. Go to **Leave** → **My Requests**
2. Find the request (must be in PENDING or APPROVED status)
3. Click **Cancel**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. If it was approved, days are restored to your balance

**Note:** You cannot cancel a request once the leave has started.

## Payslips

### Viewing Your Payslips

1. Navigate to **Payslips** from sidebar
2. You'll see a list of all your monthly payslips
3. Each payslip shows:
   - Month and Year
   - Basic Salary
   - Allowances (Housing, Transport)
   - Deductions (GOSI, etc.)
   - **Net Salary** (take-home pay)
   - Payment date

### Downloading Payslips

1. Click the **Download** button next to any payslip
2. PDF will be generated and saved to your device
3. Use this for:
   - Bank loan applications
   - Visa processing
   - Personal records

**Tip:** Download and save your payslips regularly for your records!

## Documents

### Viewing Your Documents

1. Go to **Documents** from sidebar
2. Documents are organized by category:
   - **Contracts**: Employment contracts, amendments
   - **Certificates**: Educational certificates, work experience
   - **ID Documents**: National ID, passport copies
   - **Policies**: Company policies you've acknowledged
   - **Other**: Miscellaneous documents

### Signing Documents Digitally

When a document requires your signature:

1. You'll see a notification badge on **Documents**
2. Go to **Pending Signatures** tab
3. Click **View** on the document
4. Review the document carefully
5. Click **Sign**
6. Draw your signature using mouse/touchscreen
7. Click **Confirm Signature**

**Your signature is legally binding!** Make sure you read and understand the document before signing.

**What's Tracked:**
- Date and time of signature
- Your IP address
- Your location (if available)
- Device information

## Profile & Settings

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click your name in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile**
3. You can update:
   - Phone number
   - Emergency contact
   - Address
4. Click **Save Changes**

**Note:** You cannot change your email, name, or employee number. Contact HR for these changes.

### Changing Your Password

1. Click your name → **Settings**
2. Go to **Security** tab
3. Enter:
   - Current password
   - New password
   - Confirm new password
4. Click **Change Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

## Common Scenarios

### "I forgot to check in this morning"

**Solution:**
1. Contact your manager immediately
2. Explain the situation
3. Manager can manually add attendance record
4. For future: Set a daily reminder on your phone

### "My leave request was rejected"

**Solution:**
1. Check the rejection reason in **Leave** → **My Requests**
2. Speak with your manager to understand why
3. If needed, modify dates and resubmit
4. Plan annual leave in advance to avoid conflicts

### "I need a document signed by HR"

**Solution:**
1. HR will upload the document to your Documents
2. You'll receive a notification
3. Review and sign as explained above
4. Document moves to next approver automatically

### "My payslip amount looks incorrect"

**Solution:**
1. Review the payslip breakdown carefully
2. Check for:
   - Deductions (GOSI = 9.75% of basic salary)
   - Any loans or advances
   - Attendance deductions
3. If still unclear, contact HR with specific questions

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I check in from home?**
A: No, the system verifies you're within 100m of the office location.

**Q: What if I'm sick and can't check in?**
A: Contact your manager. Submit sick leave request and provide medical certificate if required.

**Q: How long does leave approval take?**
A: Usually 1-2 business days. Plan accordingly!

**Q: Can I see my colleague's payslips?**
A: No, payslips are confidential. You can only see your own.

**Q: What if I lose my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, or contact HR.

**Q: Are my documents secure?**
A: Yes! All documents are encrypted and access-controlled. Only authorized personnel can view them.
